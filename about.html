Behind the Algorithm

Meet the developer combining institutional banking discipline with quantitative mathematics.

The Developer's Background

With over 17 years of professional experience within formal state banking and financial organizations, my entire career has been anchored in risk evaluation, systemic asset protection, and structural discipline. In the banking sector, risk is not a variable you gamble with—it is a parameter you strictly confine.

This exact institutional framework led me to algorithmic development. I realized that the retail trading world is heavily saturated with emotional biases, lagging technical indicators, and dangerous recovery strategies (like Martingale or unhedged grids) that inevitably lead to capital liquidation.

"My mission was simple: To bridge the gap between institutional risk discipline and retail spot gold (XAUUSD) trading using pure statistical mathematics."

The Evolution of Eversun

I began engineering custom scripts and market indicators using the MQL architecture back in 2019, choosing to focus exclusively on Gold due to its high volatility and liquidity characteristics. Eversun was not built in a single week or month. It is the result of continuous execution auditing, stress-testing across diverse market cycles, and cold mathematical optimization.

The current build, Eversun v12.0, represents the twelfth generation of structural refinement. Every single update over the years was deployed to achieve one critical objective: eliminating operational noise on lower timeframes (M1/M5) while keeping the absolute floating drawdown locked strictly below the 5% threshold.

Why Keep It Limited to 20 Licenses?

I am a firm believer that high-frequency mathematical anomalies in the market remain profitable only when they are under the radar. Mass-distributing a highly specialized algorithm overcrowding the liquidity pool can lead to execution slippage and reduced efficiency at broker prime-brokers levels.

By offering exactly 20 lifetime licenses, I am ensuring two things: providing an elite, institutional-grade tool to a closed circle of disciplined systematic traders, and perfectly preserving the structural execution integrity of the algorithm on global servers.
